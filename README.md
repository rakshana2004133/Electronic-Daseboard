# 📊 Electronic Sales Dashboard - Tableau Project

## 📌 Project Overview

The Electronic Sales Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using Tableau to analyze electronic product sales performance. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visual insights, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions efficiently.

This project focuses on revenue analysis, profit tracking, customer satisfaction, brand performance, product distribution, delivery analysis, and geographical sales trends.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Analyze revenue and profit trends.
* Evaluate customer satisfaction through ratings.
* Compare product and brand performance.
* Identify regional sales opportunities.
* Support strategic business decision-making.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Average Customer Rating
* Discount Percentage
* Count of Electronic Sales

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### Product Delivery Analysis

Analyzes delivery performance across different electronic products.

### Electronic Sales by State

Displays geographical sales distribution using map visualization.

### Product Distribution

Shows product contribution to total sales using a pie chart.

### Revenue by Brand

Compares revenue generated by different electronic brands.

### Profit by Brand

Highlights profitability across brands.

## 💡 Key Insights
